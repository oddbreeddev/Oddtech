The Problem

Smallholder farmers in Africa face unpredictable weather patterns and limited water resources. Traditional irrigation is labor-intensive and inefficient, often leading to crop failure or water wastage.

The Solution

Odd Agric provides affordable IoT sensors that monitor soil moisture and nutrient levels. Connected to an automated irrigation controller, the system waters crops precisely when needed, increasing yield while conserving water and power.
