The Problem

The modern world is noisy. Most people are misaligned, chasing goals that don't fit them, leading to burnout and dissatisfaction. Existing apps focus on symptoms (stress) rather than the root cause (misalignment).

The Solution

Eunoia combines journaling, cognitive behavioral frameworks, and data visualization to help you map your psyche. It's a tool for rigorous self-inquiry, designed to uncover your core values and align your actions with them.
