The Problem

Mechanization in African agriculture is expensive and often imported, meaning it isn't designed for local soil or terrain. Manual labor is inefficient and physically draining.

The Solution

Odd Bots designs rugged, solar-assisted robotic units capable of weeding, planting, and monitoring crops. Our focus is on durability, repairability, and cost-effectiveness for cooperative farming groups.
